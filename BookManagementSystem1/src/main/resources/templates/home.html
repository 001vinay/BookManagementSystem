WELCOME TO THE BOOKSTORE

A Bookstore is a collection of books, and possibly other materials and media,that is accessible for use by its members and members of allied institutions. It provides physical (hard copies) or digital access (soft copies) materials, and may be a physical location or a virtual space, or both. A Bookstore collection normally includes printed materials which can be borrowed, and a reference section of publications.
